Ancient Georgia

Georgia has a rich and ancient history, with evidence of human habitation dating back to the Paleolithic era.

Medieval Georgia

During the Middle Ages, Georgia was a major cultural and economic center, with the city of Tbilisi serving as a hub for trade and commerce.

Modern Georgia

In the 20th century, Georgia gained independence from the Soviet Union and has since become a modern, democratic nation.
